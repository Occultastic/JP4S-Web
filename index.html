About the project

The project is a game developed in Unity Engine, a popular, free-to-use, cross-platform game development engine, that takes place in a 3D world. In this game, you take over the role of a japanese schoolgirl, who was placed into the position of "Supervisor" by the schools administration after they discovered her quick-thinking and problem-solving skills as well as her athletic abilities. The game directly takes the rough setting of another game called "JP Schoolgirl Supervisor Multi" developed by "Saori Sato's Developer", which is a mobile game developed for Andoid phones and iOS phones and thus available on Google Play Store and App Store respectively.

The original game

The game will feature many of the same characters and plot points as the original one. In this case I would like to rather outline the changes that will be present instead of changes that won't be present:

  • The games artstyle will resemble the original vision of a more realistic and less saturated world.
  • The game will not feature online multiplayer.
  • The games assets will look more modern and high quality.
  • The games story will be drastically different to some degree.
