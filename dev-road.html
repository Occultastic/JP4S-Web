JP4S - Develeopment Roadmap

This page will outline the rough path of what I would implement into the game. These are not PROMISES, but I will do my best to adhere to what is written here. Over the course of the games development, things may change as I work on it.
Alpha Stage
At this point, a rough concept of the games mechanics should exist and the most essential ones should be implemented.
Some of them include:
  • Dialogue System
  • Item System
  • Task System
  • Saving and Loading Gamestate
  • Basic Movement
Beta Stage
Here, the world of the game will be build along side some of the characters. Things like the schools area and some classrooms will have to be build from scratch. As to why I won't buy assets, I would like to avoid any potential legality issues and be flexible in what I can do with this game.
Gamma Stage
Here, full focus is directed towards the NPC logic in this game. I guess for marketing purposes, I should use the hottest tech terminology out there. (as of writing this...) So here we go:
  • HOT GIRLS!!!
  • AI 1TB LOGIC
  • NO OPTIMISATION, JUST GET A BETTER COMPUTER
  • LIFE LIKE, no worries, you won't know the difference between them and real people because you don't go outside anyway
  • EVEN MORE GIRLS!!!
